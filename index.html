Your white belt stripe test will include several basic concepts and techniques that you should understand and be able to demonstrate fluently. The test will primarily focus on techniques executed from full guard, side control, and mount. You will be expected to know several guard passes, escapes, sweeps, and submissions.

    From Top Guard
  • basic guard pass
  • over / under pass
  • standing pass
    From Side Control
  • Americana
  • arm bar
  • kimura

Check out the BJJ subreddit for lots of great information and resources.
